MS DHONI

Mahendra Singh Dhoni born on 7 july 1981, he is an Indian former international cricketer and as well as captain too. he is also current captain of CSK in the IPL.

He led India to victory in three ICC trophies 2007 ICC world t20,2011 cricket worldcup and 2013 ICC champions trophy. Under his captaincy India also won 2010 and 2016 Asia cup.

He scored over 10000 runs in one day internationals,with reputation as one of the best finisher in the game. He is also one of the greatest wicket-keepers in the world.
